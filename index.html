About me


Jing Zheng is currently a master degree candidate in the School of Government, Nanjing University. Her research interest focuses on government and politics in contemporary China, nation and nationalism, and computational social science. 

Meanwhile, she is one of the persons in charge of "Zhengzhi- xueren"(政治学人)，one of the most influential commonweal platform on political science and political theory. Click here to know more.
